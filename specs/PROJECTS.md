## Stages

- `exploring` — problem not yet fully understood
- `validating` — feasibility being tested
- `deciding` — ready/not-ready decision pending
- `executing` — handed off to a builder or writer workflow
- `paused` — waiting on external dependency

## Archive Reasons

- `completed` — success criteria met, handed off
- `abandoned` — no-go decision
- `inactive` — no activity for 30+ days, no recovery plan
- `merged` — absorbed into another project
